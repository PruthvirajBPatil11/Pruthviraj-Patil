About

Undergrad by semester, systems builder by habit.

I'm a Computer Science student at Siddaganga Institute of Technology, spending most of my time outside class building things that touch AI in some practical way — an object detector that runs on a live webcam feed, a RAG pipeline that reads industrial maintenance logs, a matching engine that reads tender PDFs so humans don't have to.

I'm a Board Member of IEEE SIT SB and IEEE Computer Society, where I help run one of the largest technical fests on campus, and a member of the GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter, where I compete in and help organize contests.

Two national hackathon wins and an SIH finalist run later, I'm still most interested in the unglamorous part: making a model's output actually usable by a real person.
